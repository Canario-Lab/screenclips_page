FAQ

Short answers, because life is long enough already.

Does ScreenClips delete photos from my library?

No. Removing an item from a Clip does not delete the original from Photos.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. ScreenClips is designed as a local organizer on your iPhone.

Do I need to create an account?

No. ScreenClips does not need an account to organize your Clips.

Can I add regular photos too?

Yes. You can add screenshots and photos when they belong in a Clip.
